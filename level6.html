EWA MEMBERSHIP

“Representative-Assignment” Contract (Recruiter)
Between
EWA Membership and Independent Contractor Representative

This representative contract (the contract), executed in duplicate originals as of 1, 2020 between (hereinafter called “Rep”) and the EWA Membership (herein after referred to as “Association”).


  1. Appointment and Relationship

    2. Association hereby authorizes and appoints “Rep” as an independent contractor, to:


    1. Recruit and obtain applications for membership in the Association. Association benefits include, but are not limited to: alternative medicine, dental, discount Rx card walk-in discount card mail-order, health care, lifestyle, rewards and wellness programs issued by any insurance company (“Company”) under contract with the Association.

    2. Collect from applicants the initial contribution, the application fee and any other amounts or fees required in connection with the application (the Fees) to be tendered with such applications, and

    3. Forward all completed applications and corresponding initial contribution and fees to agency for delivery to Association or Company for review and approval/disapproval.

    Rep shall be free to exercise his or her own judgement as to the persons from whomever the rep will solicit applications for membership and as to the time, place, methods and means of each solicitation, subject, however, to the provisions of this contract. Association and Rep expressly agree the relationship between Association and Rep shall be that of an independent contractor, and nothing herein shall be construed to create the relationship of principal/agent or employer/employee.


    Rep shall have no authority to make, alter, or discharge contracts for the Association or Company; nor to waive forfeitures, grant permits, revise pricing for special risks, make endorsements or bind Association or Company in any way. Rep shall have no authority to incur any expense of liability on behalf of the Association or Company, and neither Association nor Company shall be liable for any expenses incurred by Rep in connection with Rep’s solicitation of business. Rep shall have no authority to make statements, promises or representations to an applicant that any coverage will be issued or insured with a specific effective date.


    Rep is not permitted to contact any third party administrator or third party vendor contracted by the Association or insurance carrier contracted with the Association. Association shall periodically issue instructions and procedures of a general nature concerning the performance of this contract and the relationship created between the parties. Rep shall abide by and carry out all such instructions and procedures. Association may also, from time to time, prescribe rules respecting the requirements for eligibility of applicants for coverage (not interfering with the freedom of action of Rep), which rules shall be observed and followed by Rep. Association instructions and procedures may be changed, or altered by Association in its sole discretion, at any time.


    Association and Company shall use best efforts to process applications solicited and submitted by Rep, but under no circumstances shall Association or Company have any liability for any failure or delay in processing any applications or for declining to issue a policy. With respect to any such policies as may in fact be issued, Association or Company shall have no obligation to Rep or any other person to issue any such policy or to do so with any specific effective date. It is understood that the effective date of any such policy shall be determined by Association or Company at its discretion.


    By execution of this contract Rep hereby releases Association, Company and all other individuals or entities from any and all claims, demands, or causes of action of whatever nature, which may be asserted against Association, Company or other individual or entity, and which arise from Rep’s appointments, investigation into the Rep’s background, the furnishing of information by an individual or entity pursuant to such investigation, or the monitoring of Rep’s business transactions. Rep understands and acknowledges that Association or Company may, at its sole discretion, decline to appoint Rep. Rep hereby indemnifies and holds harmless and will reimburse Association for any and all expenses, liabilities and damages, including all attorney fees and court costs, that may be incurred as a result of any action or inaction on the part of the Rep.


  2. Compensation

    3. Except as otherwise herein provided, Rep shall be entitled to and shall receive from Association compensation for Rep’s services fees based on contributions received by Association on memberships issued on approved applications secured by Rep. Such fees shall be paid as set forth in accordance with the Fee schedule as attached to this contract (Schedule A). Upon thirty (30) days written notice to Rep, Association may increase, decrease or otherwise adjust the fees and or the fee rate set forth in the fee schedule; provided, however, that any such modification shall be effective only for applications received by the Association after the effective date of such modification.


  3. Confidential Information and Trade Secrets
    4.

    Rep may have access to policyholder lists during the term of the contract. All such lists are confidential trade secrets. Rep shall not disclose such lists, directly or indirectly, or use any such trade secrets in any way. This shall apply during the term of this Agreement or at any time thereafter except as may be required in the performance of this Agreement or any time thereafter. If there is any breach of this provision, then any such fees that may be payable to Rep shall be forfeited as liquidated damages


    Unless Rep is a licensed insurance agent or licensed insurance agency, for a two-year period following termination of this contract Rep is specifically prohibited from:


    1. Inducing any policyholder to relinquish, surrender, terminate or lapse a policy or policies with Company. If Rep violates this provision, then Rep shall refund all fees paid on the related policy or policies within sixty (60) days of receipt of written notification by the Association.

    2. Disclosing or making known to any person, firm, organization or other entity any names, addresses or telephone numbers or any other information pertaining to any policyholders or customers of the Company and

    3. Soliciting any employee agent or to the affiliated representative of Association to leave his or her employment or to breach his or her Agreement with Association.

    Should Rep directly or indirectly participate in any of the actions listed above, Rep shall be subject to immediate legal action in a court of law in Westchester County, New York and shall be liable for all damages caused by such prohibited activities.


  4. Marketing

    5. Initial[]


    As an appointed agent/broker of EWA Membership products, Rep is not allowed to post, advertise or promote materials on any publicly accessible platform. This includes, but is not limited to print, internet, social media or open forums.


    Materials include, rate and benefit sheets, plan summaries, applications or any other materials specific to the plans authorized to be sold.


    Any mass emailing of plan descriptions, spreadsheets or enrollment kits is specifically prohibited.


    The personalized agent enrollment website provided by EWA Membership may be used. Emailing of the Rep’s personalized link is allowed.


    Failure to comply may result in contract termination at the option of the Association.


  5. Termination

    6. This contract shall terminate automatically upon the occurrence of any on of the following events:


    • Breach by Rep of any of the terms and provisions of this Agreement
    • Breach by Rep of any of the terms and provisions of this Agreement
    • Commitment by Rep of any acts of malfeasance, non-performance, dishonesty, or illegality
    • Termination of Rep’s appointment by Association or Company
    • The business solicited by Rep is deemed unacceptable by the Association acting at its sole and absolute discretion

    In addition to the foregoing, either party may terminate the contract at any time with or without cause by giving 30 days written notice of termination to the other party.


    Except in the case of gross negligence. Such fees are fully earned at the time of membership sale and shall continue so long as membership is in force.


  6. Non-Waiver of Provision and Severability

    7. Any failure by agency to insist upon performance of any provisions of this Agreement shall not be construed as a waiver of such provision or of the right of the Association to require performance of and to enforce all of the terms and provisions of this contract. The invalidity or unenforceability of any provisions of this Agreement shall not affect the validity of any remaining provision and all remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect and in no way shall be impaired or invalidated.


  7. Venue and Applicable Law

    8. Each party submits to the jurisdiction of the courts of Westchester County, New York for purposes of all legal proceedings arising out of or relating to this Agreement. This Agreement shall be construed under and in accordance with the law of the State of New York.
